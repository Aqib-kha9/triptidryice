COMPANY INFO

Tripti Dry Ice Co was founded 25 years ago and our growth and success during this time is a testament to the hard work and dedication from our dynamic team. We were the first company to embark distributing dry ice blocks and Nuggets in and around the local and regional market across India and quickly realised that our customers needed more than just a supplier of dry ice. As a result we have set up our OWN manufacturing units at Mahape and now at Khalapur 2 years back, both our unit has a capacity to produce 5tons & 30 tons per day subsequently.

Our Mission

"Our goal is to be the leader in every market we serve, to the benefit of our customers and our Employees."

TRIPTI Dry Ice is one of the largest MANUFACTURER and SUPPLIERS of dry ice products and has got customized delivery services in and around Maharashtra and few selected locations across India, our manufacturer unit is strategically located in metros and non metro to suffice the clients requirement in an real time basis with a bare minimum losses. We provide "Customized Packaging with Customized Distribution" to many of the world's leading Pharma, Biotech, Research Universities, Research Labs, Government Labs, Diagnostic, Blood Banks, Order Fulfillment, Home Delivery, Food Distribution, Ice Cream, Dairy, Catering and Dry Ice Blast Cleaning companies.


WHY TRIPTI DRY ICE?


True and Honest Weights
Our competitors may charge you a standard container capacity weight or what your dry ice weighed when produced, regardless of how long it may have taken to get to you. At Tripti Dry Ice, we wait until the last possible opportunity to weigh your product for a true and honest weight. When combined with our state-of-the-art containers, true and honest weights save our customers as much as 20% compared with the competition.
Superior Containers Minimize Sublimation Losses
Dry ice converts (or sublimates) from a solid to a gas from the minute it's produced. Tripti Dry Ice minimizes sublimation losses by using top quality insulated dry ice .
Extensive Distribution Network

Our dry ice production capacity of 35tons per day along with 5 branch warehouse locations allow us to meet your dry ice requirements - on time, every time.

Competitive, Stable Pricing

We price our dry ice competitively upfront without unexpected add-on charges and offer flexibility in length and terms of dry ice supply agreements.

Quality Control

To ensure our customers receive the highest quality dry ice, we video record all dry ice production and frequently test liquid CO2 and dry ice purity.

Sanitation of Equipment

Tripti Dry Ice thoroughly washes dry ice containers every time they are returned to our plants. Tripti Dry Ice performs routine cleaning on all dry ice production equipment and dies.
